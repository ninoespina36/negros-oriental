Boulevard 2025

Capturing this moment in 2025, we freeze time as a family, gathered with smiles, love, and laughter around this sculpture that marks not just a new year, but the memories we’re building together, one photo at a time.

The Pillar

Standing tall along the boulevard, this pillar is more than stone and structure. It is a silent witness to every sunrise, every passerby, and every story unfolding by the sea, reminding us that strength is built one moment, one memory, one heartbeat at a time.

Freedom

In the heart of the city lies Freedom Park, a place where the breeze carries laughter, the trees whisper peace, and every step reminds us that true freedom is found not just in open spaces but in the moments we share, the dreams we chase, and the lives we live without fear.

Downtown

In the heart of downtown where tricycles pass by and everyday scenes unfold, this photo captures the charm of simple living where smiles are genuine, time moves gently, and the rhythm of life is set by the warmth of people and the beauty in the ordinary.

Shrine

Under the quiet glow of the night, the gazebo stands as a peaceful retreat, softly lit and wrapped in stillness. This photo captures a moment of calm where time seems to pause, and the night sky gently watches over whispered conversations, quiet thoughts, and the simple magic of being present.

ILOVECHADAVALENCIA

The glowing signage of ILOVECHADAVALENCIA lights up the night with pride and warmth, a vibrant symbol of community spirit and local love. Each letter shines not just with lights, but with the stories, smiles, and soul of a town that welcomes everyone with open arms and a heart full of charm.

Fountain

Bathed in soft, colorful lights, the fountain dances quietly in the night, its shimmering waters reflecting dreams and whispers under the stars. This moment captures the magic of stillness and motion intertwined, where light and water come alive to enchant the darkness with beauty and calm.

Dagkot

“Dagkot” is a beautiful tradition of lighting candles with heartfelt prayers, a moment of quiet reflection and hope. Each flickering flame carries wishes, memories, and gratitude, illuminating the darkness with faith and the belief that even the smallest light can bring warmth and guidance to our lives.

Candles

In the sacred space of the church, candles are gently lit during the dagkot, each flame a symbol of prayer, hope, and devotion. As the soft glow fills the room, it connects hearts in silent reverence, reminding us that through faith and light, we find comfort, guidance, and the strength to carry on.

Park

Valencia Park is filled with laughter as children play on the playground, running and swinging with joy. It’s a happy reminder that simple moments often bring the greatest smiles.

Road

Along the road outside the Valencia Shrine, life moves with a gentle rhythm—tricycles passing, people walking, and the air filled with calm. It’s a place where faith meets everyday life, and every step feels connected to both the past and the present.

Road Falls

The road winding past the falls offers a breathtaking view where nature meets the journey. With the sound of rushing water beside you and the mist gently touching your skin, every turn feels like a peaceful escape into the heart of the landscape.

Sulfur Vents

The sulfur vents release steady plumes of steam from deep within the earth, a reminder of the powerful forces beneath our feet. Surrounded by the scent of minerals and the warmth of the ground, this natural wonder speaks of energy, mystery, and the living heartbeat of the land.
